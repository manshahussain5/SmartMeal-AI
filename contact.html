Is SmartMeal AI really free?

Yes! SmartMeal AI is completely free to use. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no premium features locked behind paywalls. We believe healthy eating should be accessible to everyone.

How does the AI recipe generation work?

Our AI analyzes your available ingredients, dietary restrictions, and preferences to generate personalized recipes. It uses advanced algorithms to create balanced, nutritious meals that fit your needs.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is our top priority.

Can I use it offline?

Yes! SmartMeal AI works completely offline. Once loaded, you can generate recipes, plan meals, and track nutrition without an internet connection.

How accurate is the nutrition information?

Our nutrition database is based on USDA data and is regularly updated. While we strive for accuracy, we recommend consulting with healthcare professionals for specific dietary needs.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Use the contact form above with "Feature Request" as the subject, and we'll consider your suggestions for future updates.
